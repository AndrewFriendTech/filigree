Filigree: The Student's Network

Filigree is a social media platform for students, with a plethora of tools and features to make the study life more productive and give more chances for students to socialise through the topic of their studies. We also utilise google maps in conjunction with a calendar to allow the easy planning of meetups, in addition we provide all the tool required for you to organise clubs and after class meet ups.
